    The Problem

    Police Brutality is Real

    American teens in the USA today are unaware of police brutality and how it affects them, as well as how often it occurs in their area to people just like them.

    The Video Game!

    THE HIDDEN EDUCATIONAL ASPECT

    The game is here to educate teens about police brutality. The player moves through the game avoiding gunfire from police officers, but also trying to stop them from firing by giving them flowers. If the player is hit by a bullet, they are given a fact on the subject of police brutality. The flowers represent a peaceful solution to the conflict; we do not wish to respond to violence by creating more violence.

    The Plan!

    Location Tracker

    Based on the geolocation API we will generate a map that shows the rate of police brutality around their area

    Forum

    We'll will add a commenting system so people could express their thought on a certain video

    Video Uploader

    Many times when it come to racial profiling people overlook the small conflicts that happens near their area. By adding a videos people could upload what happened to them or record someone else being brutalized nearby, to help them in a case and publicize the bad cops
